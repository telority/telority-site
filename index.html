AAACF

Framework

A governance framework built for AI augmentation — not deterministic technology.

Standard change management frameworks were designed for systems that behave predictably. AI augmentation introduces a different risk: behavioral dependency — the gradual shift of human judgment toward the AI as its influence grows. The AI Adoption & Augmentation Change Framework addresses this directly. Governance requirements, oversight design, and accountability structures scale with the actual degree of AI influence in enterprise decisions.

  • Tiered model — governance scales with AI influence, not deployment category
  • Named human accountability at every decision point
  • Designed for high-stakes, risk-sensitive enterprise environments
  • Applied where standard frameworks were not built to go
